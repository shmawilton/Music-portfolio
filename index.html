About Me

My Musical Journey

I am a dedicated flutist and music educator with over seven years of experience in performance and teaching. My journey began with the Art of Music Foundation's Ghetto Classics program, which became my sanctuary and training ground. From those humble beginnings, I've grown to become a junior tutor at Ghetto Classics, a member of prestigious orchestras including the Kenya National Youth Orchestra and Conservatoire Symphony Orchestra, and the founder of the Silva Flute Orchestra.

My passion for music is matched only by my commitment to education and community development. I believe in the transformative power of music to change lives and communities, and I've dedicated my career to making music accessible to all, regardless of their background or circumstances.

In recognition of my work and potential, I was awarded the prestigious 2026 Myrna Brown International Scholarship by the National Flute Association, which will enable me to attend and present at the 2026 NFA Convention in Portland, Oregon.

Myrna Brown International Scholarship

2026 Recipient

National Flute Association Recognition

I am honored to be the recipient of the prestigious 2026 Myrna Brown International Scholarship awarded by the National Flute Association. This scholarship recognizes my dedication to music education, my work with underserved communities through the Ghetto Classics program, and my commitment to expanding flute opportunities in Kenya.

The scholarship will enable me to attend and present at the 2026 NFA Convention in Portland, Oregon, where I will share my experiences in music education and community development. This opportunity will allow me to connect with flutists from around the world and bring back valuable knowledge to further develop the flute community in Kenya.

Year: 2026
Location: Portland, Oregon, USA
Organization: National Flute Association
