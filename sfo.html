About Silva Flute Orchestra

Our Mission

The Silva Flute Orchestra was founded with a vision to create a platform where flutists can come together to explore the rich possibilities of flute ensemble music. As the founder and director, I established this innovative ensemble to address the limited opportunities for flutists in Kenya and to expand the flute repertoire available in our country.

Our orchestra brings together talented flutists from various backgrounds, creating a unique sound that blends traditional and contemporary flute music. We are committed to showcasing the versatility of the flute family, from piccolo to bass flute, and to introducing audiences to the beauty of flute ensemble music.

Through regular performances, workshops, and collaborations, the Silva Flute Orchestra continues to grow and inspire both musicians and audiences alike.

Our Programs

Public Performances

Regular concerts featuring diverse flute repertoire, from classical to contemporary pieces, showcasing the full range of the flute family.

Educational Workshops

Masterclasses and workshops for flutists of all levels, focusing on technique, ensemble playing, and musical interpretation.

Collaborations

Partnerships with other musical ensembles, composers, and cultural organizations to create unique musical experiences.
